Intro

One crisp morning on October 8 2006, I was born in New York City. I lived in the U.S until I was 5 years old. Then my family decided it was time to leave. Right now I am 14 years old.


My family decided to live with my grandparents for a year in Asia. We left when I was 6 and moved to Canada in 2011. When I came to Canada, I missed kindergarten and senior-kindergarten and was put straight into grade 1. Luckily, my mom was a teacher and she taught me more than what I needed to know while we were travelling.



Family

I have 5 members in my family including me. There is my mother, my father, and I have 2 younger sisters. As you can tell, I am the oldest. Whoever said that being the oldest is the best, is a liar.
